## [EN-US]

I created this repository to share my knowledge with the tech community. I've learned so much from open-source content over the years, and since I've always loved teaching, this is my way of giving back!

I usually post my content and thoughts (mostly in Portuguese) on [my LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/mateus-ralves).

If you spot any mistakes, have questions, or want to suggest improvements, please feel free to reach out or open an *Issue* right here in the repository.
